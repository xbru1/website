List of English words without rhymes

Turns out certain words such as 'orange' do have some rhymes, if you want to accept how awkward they are:

Orange with Door Hinge (in certain accents)
Yttrium (pronounced yee-trium) with Liberium Arbitrium
Width with Sidth (an obsolete word for length)
Beige with Greige (a color between beige and gray)

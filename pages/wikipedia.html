List of Chess Variants

Of which there are at least 2,000. There are quite a few hilarious ones in here:

  • Kamikaze Chess: Chess, but capturing also removes the piece that performed the capture. The goal is to lose the king last.
  • Beirut Chess: Chess, but you equip a single piece with a bomb that you can detonate, removing all pieces in a 3x3 area around it, including the bomb carrier. In addition to checkmate, you can now win by exploding the enemy king.
  • Infinite Chess: Chess, but on an infinitely large board. It's slightly harder to corner the king when there are no corners.
  • Wormhole Chess: Chess played on two boards, with a wormhole in the middle to allow movement between them.
  • 5D Chess with Multiverse Time Travel: Chess, with the ability to move pieces across time and timelines. You win by checkmating a king in any time or timeline. Available as a video game, in case you are really confident you won't get a headache trying to play it.

List of English words without rhymes

Turns out certain words like 'orange' do rhyme. However, it can't be said that these rhymes are something you would normally encounter:

Orange with Door Hinge (in certain accents)
Yttrium (element, pronounced yee-trium) with Liberium Arbitrium (a legal term)
Width with Sidth (an obsolete word for length)
Beige with Greige (a color between beige and gray)
